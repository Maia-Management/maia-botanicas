Wholesale terms

Pricing

Wholesale list (~50–60% of MSRP). First order gets 10% off. Net-30 for recurring accounts (referred).

MOQ

Small MOQ per product (6–12 units for Japanese/Caribbean, 1 pack for Glassware). Shown on each product card.

Payment

Wompi (Nequi, PSE, Bancolombia, card) or wire transfer. First case: prepaid. Recurring accounts: net-30.

Delivery

🌿 In-House (Japanese / Caribbean): 3–5 business days from payment confirmation. Shipped from Santa Marta via Servientrega / Coordinadora.

🌎 Imported (Glassware): 1–2 weeks. Sourced via partners on order — we don't hold stock.

Cities served

Santa Marta, Cartagena, Barranquilla, Bogotá, Medellín, Cali. Other cities: WhatsApp for a freight quote.

Returns

In-house products: freshness guarantee. Imported glassware: factory-defect return within 7 days.

℞ Side effects may include

Menus with more personality. Bartenders running second tests. Repeat orders. Comments like "who makes this?" at neighboring tables. If Don Próspero doesn't recommend it to you, we don't sell it.
