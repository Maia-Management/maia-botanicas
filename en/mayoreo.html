Export Markets

Made in Colombia,
sent to the world

Access to three priority markets with tariff advantages and documented growth in the premium botanical ingredients segment.

🇦🇪

United Arab Emirates

Access via Colombia-UAE CEPA (active free trade agreement). Zero tariffs on Colombian food ingredients. The Gulf premium F&B market grows at 8% annually. Dubai and Abu Dhabi are distribution hubs for the entire Middle East.

✓ Tariff-free — active CEPA
🇺🇸

United States

Exponentially expanding RTD and premium mocktail market — CAGR +15% 2024-2028. Verifiable-origin ingredients with Latin American narrative are trending in premium bars in NYC, LA and Miami. Import via Miami or New York.

✓ Access via ATPDEA
🇪🇺

Europe

Gourmet distributors, hotel groups and craft bars in Spain, France, Germany and the Netherlands. Access via Colombia-EU Agreement. Verifiable-origin Colombian ingredients are in high demand in the "ingredients with story" segment dominating European craft mixology.

✓ Active Colombia-EU Agreement

Certifications & Quality

Standards the
market demands

Our current and in-progress certifications, relevant for local and international B2B purchasing.

🇨🇴

INVIMA — Instituto Nacional de Vigilancia de Medicamentos y Alimentos

Active sanitary registration. INVIMA is Colombia's FDA equivalent — certifies that our products meet food safety standards for commercialisation in Colombia and export.

✓ ACTIVE
🏭

BPM / GMP — Buenas Prácticas de Manufactura

Our production process complies with Good Manufacturing Practices under Colombian regulation (Decree 3075). Documented quality control on every batch, full traceability from raw material to finished product.

✓ ACTIVE
⚠️

HACCP — Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points

HACCP system implemented across the entire production chain. Critical control point analysis for prevention of biological, chemical and physical hazards at every stage of the process.

✓ IMPLEMENTED
🌿

ISO 22000 — Food Safety Management

ISO 22000:2018 certification in progress. International food safety management standard required by most European and US HORECA distributors for ingredient suppliers.

⏳ IN PROGRESS — 2026
☘️

USDA Organic / EU Organic

Organic certification in process for clarified concentrates line. Audit by accredited body (Ecocert / Control Union) against USDA NOP and EU Regulation 2018/848. Expected completion Q4 2026.

⏳ IN PROGRESS — Q4 2026
✡️

Kosher

Kosher certification available for clarified concentrates and artisanal syrups lines. Relevant for US distributors ($12B Jewish market) and UAE (Israel interface markets post-Abraham Accords).
