Who we are

Three people, one dispensary.

Andrew, Luz and Laura. One kitchen in Santa Marta. Four bars of our own. And a lot of jars quietly fermenting.

Short story

Maia runs four establishments in Santa Marta — El Sanatorio (immersive room + bar), Chuzo Tokyo (street-front yakitori), La Farmacia (the bar inside Sanatorio) and Sushi Pop (Rappi delivery). To stock them, we ferment koji, miso, sauces and bitters in-house.

Maia Botánicas is the distribution layer: what serves us, we sell wholesale to other Caribbean-coast bars and restaurants.

Glassware and tools we source via B2B partners (Mercado Libre Empresarial, LATAM distributors) — your bar orders, we coordinate, the partner ships direct. No warehouse. No idle inventory.

How we compound

Recipe

Small batches

Each product is made in manageable lots. No industrial scaling. If you order 12 bottles, all 12 come from the same batch.

Material

Local ingredients

Magdalena mango, Sierra Nevada coffee, Magdalena cacao, Caribbean hibiscus. We go to the market ourselves, no intermediaries.

Service

Bar-tested

Every recipe runs through Maia bars first. If it can't handle our service, we don't sell it to others.
