Our Story

Colombia importa jarabes de España.
Nosotros los fabricamos aquí.

Colombia imports bar syrups from Spain.
We make them here.

Esa contradicción es nuestra razón de existir. Colombia — el segundo país más biodiverso del planeta — importaba sus propios jarabes de bares elaborados en Europa. Incorporamos Maia Management S.A.S. el 7 de abril de 2026 para cambiar eso. Fabricamos aquí. Con botanicals colombianos. Y los mandamos al mundo — incluida España.

That contradiction is our reason for being. Colombia — the second most biodiverse country on the planet — was importing its bar syrups from Europe. We incorporated Maia Management S.A.S. on April 7, 2026 to change that. We manufacture here. With Colombian botanicals. And we send them to the world — including back to Spain.

El Hallazgo The Insight

Colombia tiene los botánicos.
Le faltaba quien los procesara.

Colombia has the botanicals.
It was missing someone to process them.

Investigando el mercado de ingredientes para bares de alta gama en Colombia, encontramos algo sorprendente: los mejores bares del país — incluyendo nuestro propio cliente ancla, El Sanatorio — compraban jarabes clarificados elaborados en España y exportados a Colombia. El precio era alto. El lead time, largo. La trazabilidad, opaca. Y el ingrediente central — la fruta tropical — crecía literalmente a minutos del consumidor final.

Researching the ingredient market for high-end bars in Colombia, we found something remarkable: the country's best bars — including our own anchor customer, El Sanatorio — were buying clarified syrups made in Spain and exported to Colombia. The price was high. Lead times were long. Traceability was opaque. And the central ingredient — tropical fruit — was growing literally minutes from the end consumer.

Cómo lo hacemos How We Do It

Manufactura por contrato,
calidad de propietario

Contract manufacturing,
owner-level quality

No necesitamos una fábrica propia para tener los estándares más altos. Operamos con equipos certificados por contrato en instalaciones INVIMA-habilitadas — lo que nos permite escalar sin los costos de capital de una planta propia, mientras mantenemos control total sobre la receta, la especificación y el control de calidad.

We don't need our own factory to have the highest standards. We operate with certified contract equipment at INVIMA-enabled facilities — allowing us to scale without the capital costs of a proprietary plant, while maintaining full control over recipe, specification and quality control.

B2B Distribution: LlevaLleva

LlevaLleva is the B2B marketplace in the Maia ecosystem where chefs and restaurants place their ingredient orders. Our products are available there for the Colombian HORECA channel — from a minimum box to a monthly kitchen subscription. Distribution follows demand; we validate the product first, then scale.

04

El Modelo Lean: Catálogo Primero

The Lean Model: Catalogue First

Somos una startup de ingredientes con filosofía lean. Este sitio web es el catálogo; el fulfillment sigue a la demanda real. No fabricamos stock especulativo — cada lote se produce contra pedido confirmado. Esto nos permite mantener la calidad absoluta por lote, ofrecer private label desde MOQ bajos, y reinvertir en desarrollo de producto en lugar de en inventario.

We are an ingredient startup with a lean philosophy. This website is the catalogue; fulfilment follows real demand. We don't manufacture speculative stock — every batch is produced against a confirmed order. This allows us to maintain absolute per-batch quality, offer private label from low MOQs, and reinvest in product development rather than inventory.
